Our Story

Shan first noticed Quinn at the new hires' table at Cafe Brock. Quinn first noticed Shan when they were waiting in line at Bethlehem's Musikfest and he asked if she had seen The Room (2003). A month later he asked her to a bar. Second date was homemade clams casino and that's when she realized they were dating. [pair with nyc pic]

The next three years were filled with adventures. Trips to NYC, Iceland, California, China. They survived their whole 2 months of long distance from Philadelphia to Austin before Shan decided to join Quinn in the Lone Star State.

They would have gotten away with even more jetsetting if it hadn't been for the coronavirus disease 2019 (i.e. COVID-19). So instead, they spent their first Austin spring locked inside and their summer learning how to camp and hike in the heat of Texas. In August they felt safe enough to take a long trip out of Texas and up to Rocky Mountain National Park.

Unbeknownst to Quinn, Shan had already procured Quinlan family ring. Halfway up a hike to the Loch, Shan stopped to catch his breath at a vista. Quinn thought he was just struggling with the altitude and tying his shoe. He was actually gathering the courage to get down on one knee and pop the question! Before he could even finish his sentence, Quinn was happy-crying and repeatedly saying "Shan! Shan!" He took that as a yes.

Travel FAQ

How do I get there?

Plane, train, or 87-octane, whatever works for you!
The wedding ceremony and reception will be held in downtown Richmond, VA. Nearest airport is Richmond International Airport (RIC): 18 minutes drive from downtown. Dulles and Reagan Airports (IAD, DCA) in Washington, D.C. are both large hubs and ~2 hour drive, depending on traffic.

The hotels suggested are < 5 miles from the ceremony location. We plan to provide transportation from ceremony to reception. The reception location is basically on the Richmond Pulse transit line (as are all the hotels we rec'd above), so you don't even need to worry about an uber home!

But where are you getting married exactly?

We don't want the whole internet to know where we'll be on April 2, 2022, so we will be keeping this information on the paper invitations sent out closer to the event.

I got here a day early, what is there to do in Richmond?

Good thing Richmond is a super cool city to explore. Here are some of recommendations from the locals (e.g. Quinn's family).

Maymont

Maymont is a beautiful public (read: free) park with acres of rolling hills, recovering raptors, a 2 story fountain, and an impressive Japanese garden. And 1 bear.

Virginia Museum of Fine Arts

Free admission and also on public transit if you are staying downtown. Exhibits change monthly, so check out their website beforehand.

BREWERIES (and bars)

If you know anything about Quinn's mom & Richmond, it's that she loves beer and the people who brew it. Many of her favorites are in Scott's Addition neighborhood such as Ardent Craft Ales, Vasen Brewing, Strangeways, and Hardywood. If breweries aren't your thing, Scott's Addition also has a cidery, meadery, arcade bars, and even a shuffleboard bar (Tang & Biscuit).
